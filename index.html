Batman

About Batman

Batman is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger and first appeared in Detective Comics #27 in 1939.

Characteristics

  • Genius-level intellect
  • Master detective
  • Exceptional martial artist
  • Stealth and infiltration
  • Utilizes high-tech gadgets

Friends of Batman

  • Alfred - Wayne Manor
  • Commissioner Gordon - Gotham City Police Department
  • Robin - Batcave
  • Catwoman - Gotham City
